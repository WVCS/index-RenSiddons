ABOUT Internet PAROBs

Internet PAROBs are PAROBs that affect the internet or computers




Description: PAROB 9 describes a computer virus created by the UN in order to keep classified documents a secret. If a file containing PAROB 9 is accessed by a computer without specific clearance, that computer will be entirely locked down, with no way to retrieve it.

History: PAROB 9 was created in an attempt to keep classified UN information a secret, every computer stationed within UN locations was given the clearance necessary to access the information stored behind PAROB 9, and it did well in this position for a number of years. However, roughly 10 years ago, the ███████ ████ managed to fake the encryption necessary to access the documents, and began to spread PAROB 9 across the internet.

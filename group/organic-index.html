ABOUT Organic PAROBs

Organic PAROBs are PAROBs that are composed of organic materials, be it flesh, plants, or viruses.




Description: Appearing as a caucasian male with many cybernetic enhancements. PAROB 1 has no need to eat or sleep, and has fully bulletproof skin. PAROB 1 also has perfect proficiency in any weapon given to it. PAROB 1 is described as having a cold demeanor when spoken to and is almost entirely focused on tactics and battles above anything else. PAROB 1 was created as a perfect soldier and was to be utilized in a variety of field missions.

History: Initially created by a member of the US government, PAROB 1 was successfully kept a secret and used in battle for over 20 years, before finally being found out by the UN. After many attempts to eliminate PAROB 1, the device that was being used to control it was severed. After which PAROB 1 fled and hasn’t been seen since.



Description: Appearing as a male human with a gray skin tone, PAROB 2 is most often seen hooked up to a variety of devices and computers, most connected to its brain. The cranial cavity of PAROB 2 is largely expanded from that of a normal human. PAROB 2’s eyes are entirely vestigial, with its eyes also adopting a gray color. PAROB 2 has a vastly increased mental capacity and far greater intelligence than is possible by any human. PAROB 2’s memory is also completely photographic, with it being able to remember and perfectly recreate an image seen for only a fraction of a second.

History: PAROB 2 was the first PAROB created directly by the UN. Created in 1946, PAROB 2 was created to store data without having to take up rooms entirely dedicated to servers. Headquartered within the UN headquarters in New York, PAROB 2 was utilized by the UN for over 50 years before suddenly gaining sentience and escaping the UN headquarters. PAROB 2 has not been seen since. PAROB 2 is considered to be a high priority target and is to be recontained at all costs.



PAROB 3 seen on an enhanced Dandelion ( Taraxacum officinale ) specimen
Description: PAROB 3 are visually nearly identical to the Western Honey Bee (Apis mellifera) , with the singular exception of being marginally larger, being similar in size to an average human thumb. PAROB 3 instances exhibit an enhanced rate and effectiveness of pollination over traditional Apis Mellifera instances. Instances of the Queen within colonies of PAROB 3 are far larger than would be expected, even given PAROB 3’s enhanced size, being close in size to an average car. Instances of PAROB 3 also have an extended lifespan, living for upwards of 10 years, as opposed to the roughly 2 months of typical Apis Mellifera instances. PAROB 3 is also able to construct far larger and far more complex structures that function as its hive.

History: PAROB 3 was created by the UN in 2010, shortly after the bee population began to decline. PAROB 3 was intended to fill the niche that would be left by the absence of bees, namely pollinating flowers. However, PAROB 3 instead created fortresses that were nearly entirely impenetrable. There are presently 4 of these fortresses known, with 2 having previously been penetrated and the PAROB 3 instances exterminated.



Description: PAROB 4 is a biological weapon that functions similarly to zombie viruses within popular media. PAROB 4 attacks the cells, causing them to rapidly decompose, including cells within the brain. PAROB 4 specifically attacks all brain cells with the exception of those that control aggression and strength. The result of this are creatures that have the appearance of a rotting corpse that is highly aggressive and dangerous.

History: Created by Russia during the Cold War as a weapon to be used would true war break out, PAROB 4 ultimately went unused.

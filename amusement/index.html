ABOUT FAZBEAR ENTERTAINMENT

"Fantasy and Fun Come to Life."

Fazbear Entertainment has lived by these words for almost 50 years.
And now, we bring you our most adventerous outing yet, Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex!

But where did we start? Fazbear Entertainment was founded in the 80s by Henry Emily. Back then, the only animatronic we had was a yellow rabbit.

From then, Fazbear Entertainment has only grown and expanded. However, poor health conditions and buildup of lawsuits caused Fazbear Entertainment to close down in the mid 90s.

But we're back!

And now, you can have fun, eat pizza, and stay at least 1 foot away from the animatronics at the Mega Pizzaplex.

ATTRACTIONS!

Whether it's bowling, blasting, or gaming, we've got something for everyone, so take a look below and see the attractions found at the Mega Pizzaplex.

Freddy, Chica, Roxxane, and Montgomery. Before, you could only see them on stage, but now, even they have downtime! Come down to rockstar row and meet the gang without the loud music or large groups. Each animatronic has their own "Greenroom" where they spend all of their downtime. And with the right passes, you can spend up to 30 minutes with your favorite gang member.

Are you ready to go on your own space adventure? Grab a Fazer Blaster, suit up, and go get those evil aliens. Just make sure, never flash your friends in the eyes. Want a break from all of the rush and loud noises throughout the pizzaplex? Well we've got you handled. Welcome to Monty Golf, where you can take a break, relax, and enjoy some nice minigolf. You could even come down to the gift shop and pick up a faz-cam! Just remember, no flash photography within Monty Golf, faz-cams will be confiscated if used.

Golfing just not your thing? Try out bowling. The time age tradition of bowling with friends and drinking and eating unholy amounts of unhealthy food and drink, now with the convienence of being in the Mega Pizzaplex! THE GANG!

You know 'em, you love 'em, but they might look different than you remember, come down to the Mega Pizzaplex and meet Freddy and Friends.

Do we even need to introduce him? Freddy Fazbear has been the face of our company for 40 years, and he's gotten many makeovers. Now, he's inspired by 80s rockstars, with some moves even inspired by David Bowie. And when you think you know everything he's got, think again. Hiding in his chest is a compartment perfeclty suited to bring around oversized birthday cakes and presents, making Freddy the perfect animatronic for your child's birthday party.

Chica the Chicken is another animatronic that came from the restructuring. Initially she was a part of her own restaurant, however she was added to the Freddy and Friends crew after Chica's Pizza World was bought out by Fazbear entertainment. Like Freddy, she's got an 80s rock makeover, and she still loves food, much like she always has. Be sure to check by Mazersize, an excersize program hosted by Chica.




But the gang isn't exactly as you last saw it. Newly added to the team is Mongomery Gator, replacing Bonnie the Bunny as the new lead bassist. Never seen before his introduction to the Pizzaplex, Monty is quickly becoming a fan favorite. He's got a fierce attidute and sharp claws, but don't fear, Monty would never hurt a guest. And remember to stop by Monty's Gator Golf and buy a Fazcam.

The second new addition to the gang is Roxanne Wolf. Taking the place of Foxy the Pirate, Roxanne is the proprietor of Roxy Raceway, where you can race alongside Roxanne and try to be the fastest, or visit the Rockstar Beauty Salon and look your superstar best. Roxanne is also the band's Keytar player. Roxanne wants to be the best in the band, and will defend her position as such ferociously.

The Daycare attendant is the manager of the Superstar Daycare, and is dedicated to keeping your children safe while you explore the Mega Pizzaplex. The Daycare Attendant has two forms, a sun form and a moon form. The sun form monitors children during playtime, with dedicated games and activities to keep your child enriched and entertained. The Moon form, which comes out when the lights are out during naptime, and will ensure your children stay asleep without any nightmares.

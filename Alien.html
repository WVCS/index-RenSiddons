    PART 1: WHY SHOULD I CARE?

    Recently, the Galactic Council has discovered a new planet with intelligent life, Earth.
    However, currently, Terrans, the natives to Earth, are not welcoming to alien life

    But why should you go to Earth?

    Terrans are one of the most generalist species in the galaxy, their
    phyiscal makeup is able to withstand extremely harsh environments
    in comparison to the tolerance ratio of other galactic speices.

    This means that, once we form an alliance with them, we may
    be able to utilize Terrans as scouts to planets otherwise
    inhospitible.

    This is a Terran. Terrans are the native speicies of Earth. They are named after
    the word for "Earth" in an ancient Terran language.

    Be careful near Terrans. Terrans in history have been extremely dangerous and
    hostile towards others, including those within their own species. This is the
    only recorded instance of intraspecies warfare in the known galaxy.

    Recommended gear when contacting Terrans:
    • Full protective suit
    • High strength tranqualizers
    • High utility first aid kit

    Do not attempt to make violent contact with Terrans in any way. As mentioned
    above, Terrans are extremely hostile and have unlocked the power of the atom.
    Contact with Terrans in general is largely discouraged.

    Seen above: A native predator to Earth

    Earth has many predators that call it home, however many
    are often weaker than others. Seen above is a species
    known as a "dog." Dogs used to be a different species
    known as a "wolf," however, during their time on the
    planet, Terrans have transformed the wolf species into
    a more friendly variant that they keep within their homes.

    There are, however, more dangerous animals on the surface
    of Earth. For example, the family Ursidae, otherwise known
    as bears, have been wholely unable to be tamed by Terrans,
    and are instead considered to be dangerous to all, particularly
    the sub-species, Ursus maritimus, who is considered one of the
    most dangerous animals on Earth.

    PART 3: UTILIZATION

    As mentioned before, Terrans can be extremely useful in exploring newly
    discovered planets within the galaxy. However, as of now, Terrans are
    not willing to accept alien life. The Cosmic Council has planned out the
    entire trajectory of Terran relations and utilization within the Galaxy:
    1. Observe. We must fully understand the behavior of terrans in
      order to attempt to make peaceful contact.

    2. Make contact. Begin with peaceful comments. Do not appear as a
      threat.

    3. Develop. Terrans have only barely managed to reach space, and sustained,
      quick missions are impossible for them. We will give them peices
      of our technology, as well as our methods to create advanced space-
      fairing vehicles.

    4. Assimilation. At this point, the Galactic Council anticipates that Terrans
      will be willing to join the Galactic Council.

    5. Utilization. At this point, the Glactic council will request something from the
      Terrans. Access to their weaponry.

      Terrans have found utilization for weapons that are capable of tearing cells apart
      at the seams and causing lifelong debilitations. The Galactic council wishes to use
      this technology in order to quell any uprisings or rebellions within the Galaxy.

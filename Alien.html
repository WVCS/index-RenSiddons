AN ALIEN'S GUIDE TO EARTH

Everything you will need to know to survive and thrive on the planet Earth.

PART 1: WHY SHOULD I CARE?

Recently, the planet Earth has been inducted into the intergalactic council.
This means that we are now allowed to access the planet and speak with it's
natives, Terrans, or, in their native tounge, Humans.

But why should you go to Earth?

Did you know that Earth is the galaxy's leading producer of Carbon?
It's true! There is more carbon on Earth than any other planet.
They have so much that it's even killing their planet. However,
we can help them.

This is a Terran. Terrans are the native speicies of Earth. They are named after
the word for "Earth" in an ancient Terran language.

Be careful near Terrans. Terrans in history have been extremely dangerous and
hostile towards others, including those within their own species. This is the
only recorded instance of intraspecies warfare in the known galaxy.

Recommended gear when contacting Terrans:
Full protective suit
High strength tranqualizers
High utility first aid kit\

Do not attempt to make violent contact with Terrans in any way. As mentioned,
Terrans are extremely hostile and have unlocked the power of the atom.
Contact with Terrans in general is largely discouraged.

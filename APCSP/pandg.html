PLAGUES AND GODS

Plauges and Gods is a soulslike game inspired by the movement and combat of Ultrakill. The player serves as a warrior plague doctor known as a cure knight, as they fight against the manifestion of plague created by a rogue sorceress. In this world, a group of powerful alchemists discovered how to turn concepts into physical things, and became a sort of god in the process. The player serves as a physical manifestation of the cure, fighting against the plague and searching to cure it.
